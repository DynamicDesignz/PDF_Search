Retraction Note: Novel two-stage surgical

severe pulmonary hypertension: a case report
Xiaochen Wu1, Jinbao Zhang1*, Hui Ouyang1, Qin Yue1 and Heng Zhao2

Retraction
This article [1] has been retracted by the Editor. Although
the patient’s legal guardian originally gave consent for
publication of the case and accompanying images, we
were informed that they withdrew this consent shortly
after publication. The article is no longer available online
in order to protect patient confidentiality.
